Design a modern, high-end B2B SaaS landing page for a company called Transformative AI.

The website should be a single-page scrolling landing page designed for enterprise audiences such as CXOs, COOs, CFOs, and manufacturing leaders.

Use a clean light theme with blue as the primary color.

The design should feel modern, futuristic, and enterprise-grade, similar to companies like Palantir, Vercel, Stripe, and Linear.

Incorporate modern UI trends including:

• Liquid glass UI elements
• Soft glassmorphism cards
• Bento grid layouts
• Subtle gradients and glows
• 3D AI / data illustrations
• Soft shadows and depth layers
• Minimal but premium typography
• Smooth section spacing
• Large visual hero area

Typography style should feel premium and tech-focused.
Use fonts similar to Satoshi, Inter, or Plus Jakarta Sans.

Primary color palette:

Primary Blue: #2563EB
Deep Blue: #1E3A8A
Light Blue Accent: #60A5FA
Background: #F8FAFC
Glass cards: translucent white with blur

Use large spacing and minimal clutter.
Design should feel strategic and enterprise, not startup playful.
